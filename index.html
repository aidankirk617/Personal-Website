Projects

BET

BET, or Beagle Encryption Tool, is a symmetric encryption/decryption tool that focuses on explaining how a particular cipher or cryptography subject works as you encrypt or decrypt it. I wanted to build something that i wish i had available to me when i first started learning about cryptography. It’s somewhat of a niche subject in computer science, and a lot of people tend to struggle with the learning curve of it. So I’m hoping BET will be able to make the subject more accessible, and bring in a wider audience to the field. Or at the very least, be a step in the right direction.

CATA-BOT

Cata-Bot is a modular discord bot created to help WCU students navigate their day to day school life with ease. By typing in a command and question prompt on the WCU Computer Science discord, Cata-Bot is able to answer it immediately. Whether it’s what time a building closes, what’s on the cafeteria menu that day, or even if there are any new events on campus you can go to.

DROPSYNC

DropSync is an automation script that allows you to sync your current DropBox directory between cloud, and local based storage. Anything you manage within the specific directory will be automatically synced and uploaded to your DropBox account, and vice versa.

PSTOCK

PSTOCK is a simple market prediction program made in Python. It uses the quandl and numpy libraries to analyze current market trends, and make an educated guess at how market trends might change in the future. Currently creating a GUI using TKINTER, and looking at how to represent the programs findings graphically using matplotlib.

BIT-SCRIPT

Bit-Script is a data grabbing automation for Bitcoin using the Blockchain API. Running a specified Bitcoin address through Bit-Script will immediately prompt you with detailed information such as the first seen date of currency, current balance, and more.
